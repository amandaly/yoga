Personal Training

Sometimes exercise will be hard, sometimes sweat will get in our eyes, and sometimes we will want to give up, but when we push past that for even five seconds we are putting in the work toward looking better and much more importantly our bodies feel better as we create a better relationship between our bodies and our minds.

Yoga Instruction

Becoming body-aware is an enlightening experience, listening to the body opens up safer and higher levels of fitness. Conscious breathing creates space within and is the most accessible path to intentional relaxation. When we relax and create opening, we can release baggage that holds us back from being true to ourselves.

Meditation

Finding stillness and settling into that stillness can be a nerve-racking prospect for some of us. When we make time for meditation we are taking responsibility of our reality, controlling the body with our mind is the first step toward controlling the thoughts in our mind. Practice is the key to enjoying our meditation and connecting to our deeper selves.
